Renowned Orange Chicken

Just the thought of a plate of sticky, sweet, and tangy Orange Chicken is enough to make anyone's mouth water!

This Orange Chicken recipe is easy to make at home and ready in 20 minutes. No need for to go orders or a hungry family waiting long, this recipe is definitley a crowd pleaser!

    Ingredients
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Cornflour
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Garlic Salt
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • 2 Oranges
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sugar
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Vegetable Oil
    How to cook the chicken
  1. We make the batter for the chicken first by mixing flour, egg, garlic salt, salt, pepper and water. Then in a separate bowl, we coat the chicken in cornflour, and add the chicken into the batter. Give it a stir to coat.
  2. Then we fry the chicken in a wok in the hot oil. My pro tip is to fry the chicken in 2 or 3 batches - this ensures all of the chicken will be lovely and crispy.
    How to make the sauce
  1. The orange sauce is made from a mixture of the orange juice and zest, sugar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger and rice vinegar.
  2. This is bubbled up in the wok until slightly thickened.
  3. The chicken is then added back in, and tossed in the sauce until fully coated.

Want to make it spicy?Add a tablespoon of sriracha to the sauce and sprinkle with a few chilli flakes before serving.

Serve the chicken over boiled rice, topped with a sprinkling of sesame seeds.

Enjoy!
