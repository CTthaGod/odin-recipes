Mom's Famous Calzones

Mom's Famous Calzones are a family favorite at every event!

These master crafted calzones are pillowy pockets full of ground beef with enough protein to keep the family full and happy for the night!

    Ingredients
  • Pizza Crust
  • Ground Breakfast Sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  1. Mix up one batch of pizza crust dough and let it rise according to the directions below, about one hour.
  2. In a large nonstick skillet combine the sausage and hambugrer with the onion. Cook until browned and then drain off grease
  3. Pour in the tomatoes and chilies and stir to combine Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly
  5. In a bowl combine the three cheeses and the egg, dash of salt and pepper
  6. Add the cooled meat mixture to the cheese mixture and stir to gently combine
  7. Divide the dough into eight pieces and roll each piece out into a circle.
  8. Spoon beef and cheese mixture onto half of the dough. You can really pile it up! Use approximately 1/8 of the mixture per calzone.
  9. Fold the other half of the dough over the top of the meat mixture so that your edges line up.
  10. Roll the edges together or use a fork to press into the edges of dough to close the calzone. Roll the dough edges.
  11. Give the beef calzone a light coat of olive oil and a sprinkle of coarse salt for extra flavor before baking.
  12. Heat oven to 450 degrees F and place the calzones on a baking sheet. Bake for about 12 minutes or until golden brown.

Enjoy!
